The Body Shape Calculator

What is this project about?

It's a calculator to determine your body shape. So you input your measurements - shoulders, waist, and hips - and it tells you which one of the five main body shapes you are according to your measurements.

Why did you want to build this project?

I love fashion and styling and I want to help others feel more comfortable when styling themselves. I have a blog where I share my style advice, which includes many articles on the importance of knowing your body type. Instead of linking to an outside calculator I thought it would be so cool if I could actually make my own!

What hurdles/problems did you run into?

The hardest part was simply figuring out how to word the if statements so they wouldnâ€™t accidentally cancel each other out. I originally had the bust measurement in there but the bust and waist measurements can be similar in some instances, which was causing a problem. When the measurements were close together it would output the wrong body shape and that's not very helpful.

How did you overcome these problems?

I tried different ways of wording the if statement to see if I could get it to work consistently but I wasn't able to make it work. Since the bust measurement isn't a crucial measurement to determining your body shape I decided to just take it out altogether.

What did you learn from this project?

Although this project is fairly simple and straightforward in terms of coding difficulty, I am proud of it because it's the first project using JavaScript I did completely on my own. I came up with the idea, the design, and did all the coding and logic for it. It gave me some confidence and reaffirmed that I was actually retaining the information I was learning. The ability to take what I learned in a class setting and implement it on my own in a real world scenario was pretty exciting and makes me want to find out what else I can make!
