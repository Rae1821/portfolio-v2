About

Problem Solving Is My Superpower.

Hello! I'm Rachel, I'm a Front End Developer with deep roots in leadership, education, and customer service. I truly believe that everything is figureoutable and I won't give up until I find a solution to a problem. While taking a break from my career to raise my son, I realized I could combine my love of problem solving with my love for helping people reach their true potential through web development.

I am passionate about building user experiences that take a client's business to the next level. Whether starting with just an idea and building an engaging user experience mobile first from scratch or taking a client's existing site and optimizing it for the modern mobile experience, I enjoy the process of bringing someone's hopes and dreams to life. I am a lifelong creative and creating something that truly helps someone else is, honestly, a dream come true.

When not at my computer, I enjoy designing & making my own clothes, hanging outside with my neighbors, playing superheros with my kiddo, or doing yoga with Waffles, our French Bulldog.
